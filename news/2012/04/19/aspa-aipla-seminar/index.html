ASPA-AIPLA Seminar

19 April 2012

ASPA were hosts to the American Intellectual Property Law Association IP Practice in the Far East Committee. We discussed the latest developments in US patent law and practice, including recent changes under the America Invents Act, USPTO statistics and recent US Supreme Court and Federal Circuit Cases.
