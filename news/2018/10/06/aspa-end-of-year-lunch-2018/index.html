ASPA End of Year Lunch 2018

6 October 2018

ASPA’s annual end-of-year festive lunch will be held this year on Monday, 10 December 2018.

  • Date: 10 December 2018

  • Time: 12 noon to 2 pm

  • Venue: Oasia Hotel Downtown, 100 Peck Seah Street, Singapore 079333

An email invite will sent shortly to all members. If you wish to attend, please RSVP as soon as possible, as spaces are limited.
