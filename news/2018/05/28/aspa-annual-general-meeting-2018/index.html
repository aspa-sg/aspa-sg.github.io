ASPA Annual General Meeting 2018

28 May 2018

An Annual General Meeting of the Association of Singapore Patent Attorneys will be held on 28 June 2018 at 6:30 pm.

  • Date: 28 June 2018

  • Time: 6 pm

  • Venue: Oasia Downtown, 100 Peck Seah Street, Singapore 079333

The AGM will be followed by a presentation by the Executive Director of IP Valuelab (IPVL), Mr Tan Shau En. A buffet dinner will follow.

Formal notice of the AGM has been sent to members.

It would help if you could please complete the RSVP for the 2018 AGM so that we may cater the right amount of food for the dinner (and have some idea of how many will turn up for the meeting).
