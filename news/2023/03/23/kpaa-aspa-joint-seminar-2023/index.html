KPAA ASPA Joint Webinar 2023

23 March 2023

The Association of Singapore Patent Attorneys (ASPA) and the Korean Patent Attorneys Association (KPAA) held a joint webinar on 23 March 2023.

The program featured presentations from Korean and Singapore patent attorneys on patent prosecution and litigation.

Programme

  • Opening by Presidents of KPAA and ASPA

  • “Preliminary Examination Program” by Mr. Lee Jaeyun (KPAA)

  • “Patent Prosecution in Singapore” by Dr Khoo Chong Yee (ASPA)

  • “Patent Prosecution In Korea” by Ms. Choi Hyun Jung (KPAA)

  • “Patent Enforcement in Singapore – New Rules of Court 2021 & New Simplified Process for IP Claims” by Ms Oh Pin Ping (ASPA)

  • Q&A and Social (20 mins)
