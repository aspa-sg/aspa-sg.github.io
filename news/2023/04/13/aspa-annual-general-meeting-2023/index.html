ASPA Annual General Meeting 2023

13 April 2023

The Annual General Meeting of the Association of Singapore Patent Attorneys will be held on 27 June 2023 at 5 pm.

  • Date: 27 June 2023

  • Time: 6 pm to 9 pm (including dinner)

  • Venue: Mastercard Asia Pacific HQ, 15th Floor, 3 Fraser Street, Duo Tower, Singapore 189352



Formal notice of the AGM will be sent to members.

It would help if you could please complete the RSVP for the Annual General Meeting so that we have some idea of how many will turn up for the meeting.
