ASPA End of Year Lunch

18 September 2015

ASPA’s annual end-of-year lunch will be held this year on Friday, 4 December 2015. More details will be available soon, but please keep the date free.

The majority of the cost of the meal for members will be subsidised by ASPA, with a nominal fee payable.

Update 1 November 2015: the lunch will be held from 12:30 pm at Zafferano. An invitation has been sent to members by email; please get in touch with the Secretary if you haven’t received yours.
