5th Global Forum on Intellectual Property 2015

7 July 2015

ASPA is pleased to be a supporting organisation of the 5th Global Forum on Intellectual Property 2015.

The GFIP brings together internationally renowned IP legal practitioners, policy makers, members of the judiciary, academics and business leaders to share insights and strategies through a series of highly focused discussions on the latest trends and issues in the highly-evolving global IP and economic landscapes. The sterling ensemble of speakers and thought-provoking plenaries and workshops at each conference has made GFIP a must-attend event for IP leaders in the region, giving participants an opportunity to meet the Who’s Who in IP from the region and beyond.

The Minister for Law will be the Guest of Honour and deliver the opening address. Other IP luminaries and policy-makers who will be speaking at GFIP 2015 include:

  • Dr. Francis Gurry, Director-General, WIPO
  • Justice Colin Birss from the UK
  • Simon Thorley, QC and International Judge from the Singapore International Commercialisation Court
  • Judicial Commissioner George Wei

Key highlights of GFIP 2015 are-

  • ASEAN Economic Integration: Interoperability and Synergies
  • Special Judges’ Plenary Session with eminent Judges from the UK, US, China, India and Singapore
  • Insights into recent high-profile IP litigation cases from around the world
  • Discussions on IP & New/Disruptive Technologies
  • Strategies for IP value creation

GFIP 2015 is also the anchor event of IP Week @ SG 2015, organized by IPOS. IP Week @ SG 2015 will be a celebration of Singapore’s achievements and milestones in IP and will showcase IP that has impacted our lives. With public talks, exhibitions and IP meeting points, this event will provide even more networking opportunities to participants and sponsors of GFIP 2015.
