Visit of Delegation From Japan Patent Attorneys Association

22 December 2015

We are pleased to announce that a delegation from the Japan Patent Attorneys Association (JPAA) will be visiting us on 20 January 2016.

As part of the visit, JPAA has kindly offered to give a talk “Outline of Patent Infringement Litigation in Japan” to ASPA members. All members are invited to attend this talk.

The talk will be held from 5 pm on 6.30 pm on 20 January 2016 at the Level 37 Conference Room of Spruson & Ferguson (Asia), at 152 Beach Road, Gateway East, Singapore 189721.

If you are interested in attending the event, please get in touch with the Secretary by 31 December 2015 to register. We look forward to seeing you at the event.
