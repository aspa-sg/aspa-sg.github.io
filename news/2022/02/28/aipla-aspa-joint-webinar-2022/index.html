AIPLA ASPA Joint Webinar 2022

28 February 2022

The Association of Singapore Patent Attorneys (ASPA) and the American Intellectual Properly Law Association (AIPLA) are pleased to present a joint webinar, to be held on 9 March 2022 at 9:00 am Singapore Time.

The program will feature presentations from United States and Singapore patent attorneys on patent prosecution and litigation.

The webinar is free for ASPA members. If you’re not a member yet, you can apply to join ASPA.



AIPLA ASPA Joint Webinar Details

  • Date: 9 March 2022

  • Time: 9 am to 10:30 am

  • US and Canada: 8 March 2022 from 8 pm to 9:30 pm (Eastern Standard Time)

  • Platform: Zoom

Programme

  • “Recent Developments on Patent Prosecution in the US” by Mr. Michael Craig (AIPLA)

  • “Patent Amendments: Best Practices to Avoid Hazards” by Dr Chong-Yee Khoo (ASPA)

  • “How to Increase Your Odds in US IP litigation, and How to Reduce Costs” by Mr. Patrick Coyne (AIPLA)

  • “Key features of patent litigation in Singapore” by Ms Oh Pin-Ping (ASPA)

  • Q&A and Social
