JPAA ASPA Joint Webinar 2022

15 February 2022

The Association of Singapore Patent Attorneys (ASPA) and the Japanese Patent Attorneys Association (JPAA) are pleased to present a joint webinar, to be held on 22 February 2022 at 11:00 am Singapore Time.

The program will feature presentations from Japanese and Singapore patent attorneys on patent prosecution and litigation.

Please read on for full details of the event or click the button to register.

JPAA ASPA Joint Webinar Details

  • Date: 22 February 2022

  • Time: 11 am to 12:30 pm Singapore time (12 pm to 1:30 pm Japanese time)

  • Venue: Webinar details will be sent out closer to the date

Programme

  • 11:00 am to 11:05 am: Opening (Ms Low Pei Lin, ASPA President)

  • 11:05 am to 11:20 am: Presentation on Patent Prosecution by Mr. Mori Tomohiro (JPAA)

  • 11:20 am to 11:35 am: “Patent Amendments: Best Practices to Avoid Hazards” by Dr Chong to Yee Khoo (ASPA)

  • 11:35 am to 11:50 am: “Procedures for filing patent applications in Japan” by Mr. Kawabata Koji (JPAA)

  • 11:50 am to 12:05 am: “Key features of patent litigation in Singapore” by Ms Oh Pin to Ping (ASPA)

  • 12:05 am to 12:25 am: Q&A and Social (20 mins)

  • 12:25 am to 12:30 am: Closing (Ms Low Pei Lin, ASPA President)
