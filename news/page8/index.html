ASPA News

  • 13 April 2010

    Letter to IPOS on SCOPE IP License

    ASPA wrote to IPOS on Clause 5.2 of the SCOPE IP licence.

  • 2 February 2010

    Changes to Patent Agent Rules and New Code of Conduct

    ASPA organised a discussion session on the recent changes to the Patent Agent Rules and the introduction of a new Code of Conduct for patent attorneys in Singapore.

  • 15 October 2009

    Letter to IP Australia on Flexible Search and Examination

    ASPA made submissions to IP Australia on its consultation paper of August 2009 on flexible search and examination.

  • 3 September 2009

    Letter to IPOS on Proposed Changes to Patent System

    ASPA made submissions to the Registrar of Trademarks and Patents at the Intellectual Property Office of Singapore (IPOS) on the consultation paper dated 3 July 2009 on proposed changes to the patent system.

  • 9 May 2009

    Seventh Annual General Meeting

    ASPA held its 7th Annual General Meeting today.
