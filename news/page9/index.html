Activities and Upcoming Events

ASPA News

  • 19 July 2004

    Changes to the Singapore Patents Act

    Mr. Keith Callinan gave a talk on recent changes to the Singapore Patents Act.

  • 21 May 2004

    Second Annual General Meeting

    ASPA held its 7th Annual General Meeting today. At the meeting, the Association established a Code of Conduct for Singapore patent agents.

    Dr Wolfram Schiweck gave an introductory talk on biotech and nucleic acid inventions.

  • 31 October 2003

    Recent Developments in IP Laws in Europe, Singapore and Asia

    Seminar on “Recent Developments in IP Laws in Europe and Singapore/Asia” jointly organised by the IP Academy and ASPA. Speakers were Michael Fysh QC and Tan Tee Jim SC.

    About 50 people from industry and the legal and patent professions attended the talk.

  • 31 October 2003

    MOU With the IP Academy

    ASPA has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with the IP Academy.

    The objectives of the memorandum include:

    • the organisation of joint academic activities, such as seminars, workshops, symposia and lectures/tutorials, in particular on patent drafting and interpretation and patent search and examination procedure; and

    • the exchange of training manuals, publications and other materials of common interest.

  • 10 September 2003

    Recent Developments in US Patent Law

    Marc Bobys gave a talk on recent developments in US law relating to patent prosecution. Mr Bobys spoke on the doctrine of equivalents, file wrapper estoppel as well as discussing the Festo decision.
