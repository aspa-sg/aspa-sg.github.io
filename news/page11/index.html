Activities and Upcoming Events

ASPA News

  • 29 May 2003

    First Annual General Meeting

    ASPA held its 1st Annual General Meeting today. The Constitution of the Association was amended at this meeting.

  • 13 May 2003

    Review of the Singapore Patent System

    Mr Tan Tee Jim, S.C., Mr Jupiter Kong and Mr Adam Bogsch represented ASPA at a dialogue with IPOS.

  • 12 March 2003

    Trends in the Patent Agent Profession

    Mr. Max Waldbraum gave a talk on “Emerging Trends in the Patent Agent Profession”.

  • 13 February 2003

    Letter to IP Academy on the Patent Agent Profession

    ASPA wrote to the IP Academy to encourage government support for the development of Singapore’s patent profession.

  • 1 February 2003

    Press Release on the Establishment of the Association of Singapore Patent Agents

    ASPA issued a press release this month announcing its establishment.
