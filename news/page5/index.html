Activities and Upcoming Events

ASPA News

  • 19 April 2012

    ASPA-AIPLA Seminar

    ASPA were hosts to the American Intellectual Property Law Association IP Practice in the Far East Committee. We discussed the latest developments in US patent law and practice, including recent changes under the America Invents Act, USPTO statistics and recent US Supreme Court and Federal Circuit Cases.

  • 10 April 2012

    Feedback on Proposed Amendments to Patents Act to Liberalise the Patent Agent Sector

    ASPA wrote to the Ministry of Law with its feedback on the Ministry’s proposals to amend the Singapore Patents Act to liberalise the patent agent sector.

  • 18 July 2011

    Letter to IPOS on Review of the Patent Agent Regime

    ASPA wrote to IPOS on its plans to review the patent agent registration regime in Singapore.

  • 3 May 2011

    Ninth Annual General Meeting

    ASPA held its 9th Annual General Meeting today.

    The after dinner speaker was Mr. Duncan Hart, who gave a talk on “Patent Attorney/Agent Business Morals: The Good, the Bad and the Ugly”.

  • 6 April 2011

    Inventive Step in Singapore

    Mr. Tibor Gold gave a talk on inventive step in Singapore after Mühlbauer in the light of recent decisions in the UK Courts.
