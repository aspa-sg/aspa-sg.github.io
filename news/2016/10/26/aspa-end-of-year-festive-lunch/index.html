ASPA End of Year Festive Lunch

26 October 2016

Updated 22 November 2016

This event has sold out. We look forward to seeing you at a future ASPA event.

ASPA’s annual end-of-year festive lunch will be held this year on Friday, 2 December 2016.

  • Date: 2 December 2016

  • Time: 12 noon to 2 pm

  • Venue: Jamie’s Italian, 1 Harbourfront Walk, #1-165-167 VivoCity, Singapore 098585

  • Cost: $20 members

Please come early to catch up with your friends before 2016 ends. A three course will be served around 12:30 pm.

An email invite has been sent to all members. If you wish to attend, please RSVP to Howard by 28 November 2016. As spaces are limited, please RSVP as soon as possible.
