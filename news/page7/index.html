  • 18 July 2011

    Letter to IPOS on Review of the Patent Agent Regime

    ASPA wrote to IPOS on its plans to review the patent agent registration regime in Singapore.

    Ninth Annual General Meeting

    ASPA held its 9th Annual General Meeting today.

    The after dinner speaker was Mr. Duncan Hart, who gave a talk on “Patent Attorney/Agent Business Morals: The Good, the Bad and the Ugly”.

    Inventive Step in Singapore

    Mr. Tibor Gold gave a talk on inventive step in Singapore after Mühlbauer in the light of recent decisions in the UK Courts.
