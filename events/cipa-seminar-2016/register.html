ASPA Intellectual Property Seminar with CIPA

Wednesday, 5 October 2016 from 12 noon to 5 pm at Pacific 1 Ballroom, Pan Pacific Hotel

Full details on the ASPA CIPA IP Seminar event page

Conditions of Registration

  • The number of places at this event is limited and registrations will be accepted in the order they are received
  • If you wish to attend, you are required to register beforehand. No on-site registrations will be permitted
  • The registration fee for members of ASPA is S$30 and the registration fee for non-members of ASPA is S$120
  • Payment for the registration fee may be made by cheque payable to "The Association of Singapore Patent Attorneys" or in cash at the event
  • If you register for the event and don't attend, a fee of S$120 will be charged. This applies to both members of ASPA and non-members of ASPA
  • Up to 3 public CPD points are available for this event
  • Participants who wish to claim CPD Points are reminded that they must comply strictly with the Attendance Policy set out in the CPD Guidelines. This includes signing in on arrival and signing out at the conclusion of the activity in the manner required by the organiser, and not being absent from the entire activity for more than 15 minutes. Participants who do not comply with the Attendance Policy will not be able to obtain CPD Points for attending the activity. Please refer to www.sileCPDcentre.sg for more information
